SCORE: Players earn 1 point per level for each point of speed (if speed is set to 5, then player earns 5 points), multiplied by 1.5X if Reverse is on and another 1.5X if the timer is on.
High Score 0
Reverse
REVERSE: Click to the right to turn on reverse. If reverse is on, player must enter the pattern backwards. For example, if the game flashes 'red', 'blue', 'green' then the play must enter 'green', 'blue', then 'red'. Points are multiplied by 1.5X
Timer
TIMER: Click to the right to turn on the timer. If the timer is on, player must start entering the pattern within 10 seconds and points are multiplied by 1.5X
Speed: 5
SPEED: Move the slider to the right to increase or decrease the speed of the light pattern. The speed automatically increases at level 6, 10, and 14. Speed values range from 1-9. Player gets one extra point for every level of speed times possible multipliers for reverse and timer

Game Rules
1. Press the START button. Simon will give you two signals. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color lens.
2. Simon will duplicate the first two signals and add one. Repeat these three signals by pressing the same color lesnes, in order.
3. Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly. After the 5th, 9th, and 13th levels, Simon automatically speeds up.
5. If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, or if you take more than 10 seconds to start entering the signal (if the timer is on), this means you've lost.
